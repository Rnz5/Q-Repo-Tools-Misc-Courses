# Coursera

## Quick Info
- **Main Catalog URL:** https://www.coursera.org/courses?query=free
- **Access Model:** Free audit mode (access to videos/readings, no certificate)
- **Provider Type:** Platform Aggregator (hosts courses from universities & companies)
- **Certifications:** Paid

## What You'll Find
Thousands of courses from top universities and companies:

- **Career Development:** Resumes, interviews, LinkedIn, job search
- **Business:** Entrepreneurship, management, marketing, finance
- **Professional Skills:** Communication, negotiation, leadership
- **Personal Development:** Psychology, wellness, academic subjects
- **Technology:** IT support, data analysis, software tools
