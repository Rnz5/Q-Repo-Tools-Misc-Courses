### Common Searches
When visiting these catalogs, search for:
- "Sheets" or "Spreadsheets" for Excel/Google Sheets training
- "Docs" or "Word" for word processing and document creation
- "Slides" or "PowerPoint" for presentation design
- "Calendar" or "scheduling" for time management tools
- "Collaboration" or "teamwork" for group tools and workflows
- "Email" or "Gmail/Outlook" for email management
- "Drive" or "cloud storage" for file management
- "Meet" or "Zoom" for video conferencing

### Quick Tips
1. **Start with basics** if you're new to a tool, then progress to advanced features
2. **Look for "certification" paths** if you want official recognition (especially for Google and Microsoft)
3. **Check update dates** - office tools change frequently, ensure you're learning current versions
4. **Practice immediately** - apply new skills to real work tasks for faster learning
5. **Learn integration** - how different tools work together (e.g., Google Sheets with Docs)
