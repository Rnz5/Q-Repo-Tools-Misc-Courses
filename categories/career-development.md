### Common Searches
When visiting these catalogs, search for:
- "Resume" or "CV" for resume writing and formatting
- "Interview" or "interview preparation" for interview skills
- "LinkedIn" or "personal branding" for online presence
- "Career change" or "transition" for switching industries
- "Salary negotiation" or "compensation" for negotiation skills
- "Job search" or "hunting" for job finding strategies
- "Cover letter" or "application" for application materials

### Quick Tips
1. **Build a portfolio** - create work samples to showcase your skills
2. **Network strategically** - use LinkedIn to connect with industry professionals
3. **Customize materials** - tailor resumes and cover letters for each application
4. **Practice interviews** - conduct mock interviews to build confidence
5. **Track applications** - maintain records of jobs applied to and follow-ups needed
