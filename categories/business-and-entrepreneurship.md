### Quick Tips
1. **Validate ideas early** - test business concepts before major investment
2. **Network with mentors** - connect with experienced entrepreneurs for guidance
3. **Start small, iterate** - launch minimum viable products and improve based on feedback
4. **Track metrics** - monitor key performance indicators from day one
5. **Learn from failures** - analyze what doesn't work as valuable learning opportunities
